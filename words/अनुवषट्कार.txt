(-रः) A second वषट्कार or exclamation at a sacrifice, the first वषट्कार being intended for the principal deity and the अनुवषट्कार for the other deities; in the Jyotiṣṭoma, for instance, the Chamasas with the juice of the Soma plant are at the वषट्कार offered to Indra, and afterwards at the अनुवषट्कार to the other deities, Mitra and Varuna &c.
