A curvature of the spine, considered as one of the diseases produced by the derangement of the temperamental element <i>air</i> (see वातव्याधि) and thus described by <i>Suśruta:</i> अङ्गुलीगुल्फज- ठरहृद्वक्षोगलसंश्रितः । स्नायुप्रतानमनिलो यदि क्षिपति वेग- वान् । विष्टब्धाक्षः स्तब्धहनुर्भग्नपार्श्वः कफं वमन् । अभ्यन्तरं धनुरिव यदि नमति मानवः । तदा सोऽभ्यन्तरायामं कुरुते मारुतो बली ॥ ‘When deranged <i>air</i> pervading fingers, ankles, belly, heart, chest and neck, contracts the muscles (of these parts), when the eyes and jaws of the patient become fixed and his sides (at it were) broken, when he vomits phlegm and his body is <i>bent inwards</i> (i. e. forwards), such deranged <i>air</i> produces the disease <i>Abhyantarā-</i> <i>yāma</i>’.
